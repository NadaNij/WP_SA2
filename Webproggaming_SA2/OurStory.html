Our Story

Founded in 1999,Naosu Healthcare Plus emerged from the collective passion of a team of young doctors aiming to bring affordable healthcare to people. The name Naosu came from the japanese word '直す' meaning cure. Our young medical students graduating from the Japan Medical University had a simple goal in mind: make healthcare accessible to everyone! Now almost three decades later,there are 15 branches of Naosu clinics, pharmacies and hospitals providing the best healthcare to public!
